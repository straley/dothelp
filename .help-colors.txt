[:bold:]dotHelp Colors[:normal:]

To add colors and bolding to your [:bold:]dotHelp[:normal:] files, use any of the following codes:

[:bold:]Bolding[:normal:]
\[:bold:\] to make things [:bold:]bold[:normal:]
\[:normal:\] to make things not bold

[:bold:]Colors[:normal:]
\[:black:\] to make the foreground text black ([:black:]sample[:reset:])
\[:red:\] to make the foreground text red ([:red:]sample[:reset:])
\[:green:\] to make the foreground text green ([:green:]sample[:reset:])
\[:yellow:\] to make the foreground text yellow ([:yellow:]sample[:reset:])
\[:blue:\] to make the foreground text blue ([:blue:]sample[:reset:])
\[:magenta:\] to make the foreground text magenta ([:magenta:]sample[:reset:])
\[:cyan:\] to make the foreground text cyan ([:cyan:]sample[:reset:])
\[:white:\] to make the foreground text white ([:white:]sample[:reset:])
\[:reset:\] to make the reset foreground text to the system default.

\[:BLACK:\] to make the background text black ([:BLACK:]sample[:RESET:])
\[:RED:\] to make the background text red ([:RED:]sample[:RESET:])
\[:GREEN:\] to make the background text green ([:GREEN:]sample[:RESET:])
\[:YELLOW:\] to make the background text yellow ([:YELLOW:]sample[:RESET:])
\[:BLUE:\] to make the background text blue ([:BLUE:]sample[:RESET:])
\[:MAGENTA:\] to make the background text magenta ([:MAGENTA:]sample[:RESET:])
\[:CYAN:\] to make the background text cyan ([:CYAN:]sample[:RESET:])
\[:WHITE:\] to make the background text white ([:BLACK:]sample[:RESET:])
\[:RESET:\] to make the reset background text to the system default.
